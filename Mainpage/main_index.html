Frequently Asked Questions

1. How many apps can I store?
There is no limit for storing info.
2. What happens if I forget my master password?
For security reasons, your master password cannot be recovered. You’ll need to reset your vault and create a new one. This ensures that only you ever have access to your data.
3. Does Pass Vault auto-fill my passwords?
Yes, Pass Vault can auto-fill credentials in supported browsers and apps, saving you time while keeping your login process secure.
4. Is my data secure in Pass Vault?
Yes. All your data is encrypted and protected so that only you can access your vault.
